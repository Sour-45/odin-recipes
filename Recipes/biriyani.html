A biriyani is a cooked rice along with variety of spices addded to it. It may have a veg option,egg or other cosnumed meat options

One of the most popular dishes in India and West asian counties. Cook rice, add the spice mix onions,tomato,garlic,other spcies etc., then the cooked meat.

  • Basmati rice
  • Onions
  • Spices
  • other veggies
  • Chicken

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. add rice to it and wait 12-15min till it is cooked
  3. Separate pot add oil,garlic,spices,ginger,chilles,etc. then cook it well
  4. add chicken to it, mix it, wait till it is cooked properly
  5. Mix the rice to the pot
